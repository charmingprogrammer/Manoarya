Know About Science

Science is a careful and defth observation of natural phenomenon.

Word science come from Latin verb 'Scientica'.
Scientica meaning id 'To know'.

Scientific methods

Method or rules of scientific research is called scientific method.

Some scientific method are following.

1. Systematic observations
2. Controlled experiments.
3. Qualitative and quantitative reasoning
4. Mathematics modeling
5. Predictions
6. Verificationof theory

To apply one science theory or formula. First check that this theory of formula is complete all the steps of scientific method and also philosophical debate by many scientists and philosophers.

Natural Science

What we study in physics chemistry and biology all the stuff known as natural science.

Physics : Physics is the study of nature and its law.

Word physics comes from Greek Word which meaning is natural.

Two principles of physics.

1. Unification
2. Reduction

Unification

Try to explain and understand natural phenomena in same theory or formula.

Reduction

Try to explain complex natural phenomena to simpler way known as reduction.

Scope and craze of physics

Two domains for scop of physics

1. Macroscopic
2. Microscopic

1. Macroscopic : The macroscopic domain includes phenomena at laboratory and astronomical scales.

2. Microscopic :The microscopic domain includes atomic, molicular and nuclear phenomena.

Classical Physics

Classical physics deals with macroscopic phenomena and include subject like mechinics, electrodynamic, optics and thermodynamic.

Fundamental forces in nature.

1. Gravitational Forces
2. Electromagnetic Forces
3. Strong Nuckear Forces
4. Weak Nuclear Forces

1. Gravitational Forces

The gravitational force is the force of mutual attraction between two objects by property of their masses.

Gravitational Force is the universal force.
Means, every object on the universe experience gravitational force.

2. Electromagnetic Force

The Electromagnetic Forces is the force between charged particles.

Electromagnetic forces are more powerful as compair to gravitational force.

Gravity is always attract, but Elcotomegnatic force can be attract or repulse.

3. Strong Nuclear Force

The strong nuclear force binds protons and neutrons in a nucleus.

Strong Nuclear Force is the strongest force as compair to all fundamental forces.

4. Weak Nuclear Force

The weak nuclear force appears only in certain nuclear process such as the B-dacay of a nucleus.

The Weak Nuclear Force is the weak nuclear force but not weak from gravitational force.

    What is difference between Machine learning and Data Science?

    Category : Explanation

    Machine learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence that focuses on the development of algorithms and statistical models that allow computers to learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data. It involves using large amounts of data, as well as computational and statistical methods, to discover patterns and make predictions or decisions.

    Data science is a broad field that involves using a range of techniques, including machine learning, to extract insights and knowledge from data. Data science includes a wide range of activities, such as collecting, cleaning, and preparing data; visualizing and exploring data; building and evaluating machine learning models; and communicating findings and recommendations based on data.

    In summary, machine learning is a specific approach to solving problems with data, while data science is a broader field that includes machine learning as one of its tools.

    Tips For New Developers By Manoarya.

    Category : Explanation

    Hi what's us geeks today in this post we are going to share with you the top five tips for new developers who just start coding.

    So are you geeks excited?

    Let's go

    1. You have your reason and remember it.

    2. Follow the coding community.

    3. Don't hesitate to google your problems.

    4. Struggle to solve problems but ...

    5. Compare you to only you

    1. You have your reason and remember it.

    Geeks you have a strong reason and you need to remember it when you are coding and after some time you become frustrated.

    So remember your reason and start coding with full excitement.


    2. Follow the coding community.

    Why are you frustrated when coding? Because you don't solve your coding problems right. That's the reason why I started following the coding community.

    Some coding communities are followed.

    1. Stackoverflow

    2. GFG

    3. Dev.to


    3. Don't hesitate to google your problems.

    You already know google is the smartest platform to solve any problem. So, why do you hesitate to search your problem on google?

    Believe me guys, please search your coding problems on Google. It will help you so much.


    4. Struggle to solve problems but not too much.

    Geeks, your life is very important and live your life with happiness.

    Please don't Struggle day to night to solve coding problems.

    I see lot's of new developers who solve coding problems and forget all the routines.

    Like they don't exercise, don't sleep and don't meet up with family and friends. Only sit on the front of the computer.

    After some days it's become a habit and even I say bad habits. Because of that you become sick and lose your interest in coding.

    So please develop a routine which involves exercise time, sleeping time and other more important tasks. And don't forget to follow that routine.


    5. Compare you to only you.

    Everybody in this world compares themselves to others. They think I am stupid and other is intelligent.

    As a geek we don't need to Compare to other geeks. Only Compare you with only you.

    Only matters that you are better than the day before.

    You need to be inspired by other developers but you don't need to compare.

    So only focus on you.

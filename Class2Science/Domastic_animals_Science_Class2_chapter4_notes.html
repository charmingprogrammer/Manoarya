Domestics Animals

Animals that kept at our home and in farms are called Domestics Animals.

Example : Cows, dogs, horses, hens, goats ......etc.

Domestics animals give us so many things like food, fibres, and work for us.

Animals give us food.

1. Milk : Cow, goat, buffalo
2. Eggs : Hen, duck, turkey
3. Meat : Hen, goat, duck
4. Honey : bee

Animals give us things like :

1. Leather :
Skin of animals like buffalo, sheep, lamb, cow ...etc. used as leather.
Leather is used for making shoes, bags, belts, purses, etc.

2. Wool :
We get wool from sheep to make woollen clothes.
Woollen clothes keep us warm in winter.

3. Silk :
Silk fibre get from silk cocoons.
Silk cocoons made by silk-warm.
Silk thread used to make silk cloths.

Animals like horses, bulls, asses, camels ..etc carry loads for us to make our work easier.

2. Pet Animals

Animals that kept at home for entertainment called pet animals.
Example : Dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, etc.

Care of Domestics Animals

1. They are washed an kept clean.
2. They are protected from cold, heat and rain.
3. They are given proper food.
5. We build shelter for them.

Shelter for Domestics Animals

1. Cages(Enclousers) : Hens, sheeps, goats.
2. Stables : Horses.
3. Kennels : Dogs.
4. Sheds : Cows, bulls, buffalos.

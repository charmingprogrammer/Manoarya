Uses of plants

Plants provide us so many things in our life like food, medicines, clothing and shelter.

Let's learn how plants help us in our life.

Provide Food

Roots : Carrot, Radish, Beetroot.

Stems : Potato, onion, sugarcane

Leaves : Cabbage, Lettuce, Spinach

Flower : Cauliflower, Broccoli

Fruits : Apple, Orange, Pomegranate

Seeds : [Cereals : Wheat, Rice, Maize, Jowar, Bajre.]
[Pulses : Gram, Beans, Peas, Soybeans]
[Oils : Sunflower oil, Mustard oil, Groundnut oil]
